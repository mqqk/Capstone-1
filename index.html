FBI Crime Data


Crime Statistics from the FBI

This Application is designed to provide you with particular categories by which to search for specific crime stats.

An important note: not all statistics are available at all times. Depending on the jurisdiction, state, or year, the FBI may not have the statistics for a variety of reasons.

A Short History

Initially most reporting agencies were from large cities, and before 1957 the FBI did not aggregate data, even to the national level.

Over the years the number and types of agencies reporting crime have increased.

Every agency that reports to the UCR is assigned a unique Originating Reporting Agency Identifier or ORI.
