Short History

Initially most reporting agencies were from large cities, and before 1957 the FBI did not aggregate data, even to the national level.


Over the years the number and types of agencies reporting crime have increased.


Every agency that reports to the UCR is assigned a unique Originating Reporting Agency Identifier or ORI.
